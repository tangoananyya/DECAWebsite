About the Chapter

Built to
Win

Turner Fenton DECA is one of the largest, most accomplished chapters within Ontario DECA. Each year, over 400 students compete for roughly 100 spots in the chapter — and those who make it represent our school in business management, finance, marketing, entrepreneurship, and hospitality.

Since 2017, we've earned over 1,500 awards, qualified 100+ students for ICDC, and produced multiple world champions. In 2025 alone: 40 provincial awards and 8 ICDC qualifiers.
