Frequently

Who is eligible to apply, and when does the process open?
All students attending Turner Fenton for 2025–2026 are eligible. Register here: forms.gle/uGT5vVqKD2Srmwhg9
How do I join TFSS DECA as a general member?
Three steps:
  • Take the 50-question multiple-choice Entrance Exam.
  • Top scorers advance to a Case Study round — 10 min prep, 2–3 min presentation to the exec team.
  • Successful candidates receive an offer via Google Form.
Can I compete in both Writtens and Orals?
Members compete in either Writtens or Orals — not both. For additional competition options, see DECA Online Events.
Are there preparation resources for the Entrance Exam?
Yes. Access the full prep resource folder here: Google Drive
What does the Entrance Exam involve?
50 multiple-choice questions in the same format as official DECA competitive exams. Focus on preparation — the number of applicants varies each year but is always competitive.
What competition categories can members participate in?
Five clusters: Marketing, Finance, Hospitality & Tourism, Entrepreneurship, Business Management & Administration. View Cluster Poster →
